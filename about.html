About

4 Oranges

Established in 2004, 4 ORANGES has become the leading company of the decorative paint market in Vietnam.

4 ORANGES is a foreign capital enterprise and has invested in the Vietnamese decorative paint market since 2004. Considered as one of the largest paint factory in Southeast Asia, the factory is located at Duc Hoa 1 Hanh Phuc Industrial Park, Duc Hoa District, Long An, Vietnam.
