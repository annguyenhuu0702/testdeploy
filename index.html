About

COMPANY INFORMATION

Company 4 Oranges Co., Ltd is a 100% foreign invested company belonging to ASIA LEADER INTERNATIONAL INVESTMENT group.

Identifying Vietnam as a potential market and a foundation for development to other countries in the region. In 2004, 4 Oranges Company invested in a modern technology line with a production capacity of 100 million liters of paint and 60 thousand tons of putty per year, with a cost of 14.5 million USD at Duc Hoa 1 Industrial Park, Long An province, this is one of the three largest paint factories in Southeast Asia today.
